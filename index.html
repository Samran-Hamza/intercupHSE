About the Tournament

The legendary football tournament for international students is back! For the third year, we are organizing the biggest sports event for HSE international students.

Grab your boots, warm up your ankles, and register for the tournament by April 10 at 23:59.

Come defend your faculty's honor, play, and win – the next tournament will take place on May 18, 2025.

Don't miss the best project for international students!
(Awarded at the III Intercampus Spartakiad of HSE)

Your To-Do List:

1
Are you an international student, postgraduate, or preparatory course attendee for international citizens? Register by April 10, 2025. Use your @edu.hse.ru email when registering.
2
Wait for registration confirmation by April 15, 2025. Join your team/faculty's Telegram chat upon invitation from the organizers.
3
Attend team training sessions provided by the Physical Education Department. Stay updated via the website and Telegram chats.
4
Come to the match on May 18, 2025, give it your all, showcase your skills, be part of an international event, and make new friends!
